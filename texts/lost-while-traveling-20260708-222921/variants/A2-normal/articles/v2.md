# Lost in a Small Town

Mia was on holiday in a small town in Europe. She wanted to find a famous cafe. She walked and walked, but her phone's map app suddenly died.

Mia looked around. All the streets looked the same. She walked left, then right, then left again. Her feet hurt, and her heart began to beat fast. Where was she?

Then she walked into a quiet street. She had never been there before, and she felt more and more nervous. Nobody was there. Mia stopped, took a deep breath, and tried not to worry.

Suddenly, a woman with a small dog walked by. "Hi there! Are you lost?" the woman asked kindly. "Yes," Mia said. "I can't find a cafe near here. I'm a little scared." The woman smiled softly and said, "Don't worry. I know a shortcut. Come with me!"

They walked together and talked. Mia did not feel scared anymore. The woman lived in the town, so she knew all the streets very well. Soon, they reached the cafe.

Inside, Mia thanked the woman and bought her a cup of tea. The owner laughed and said, "She comes here every day! You made a new friend today." Mia laughed too. Getting lost was not so bad after all.
