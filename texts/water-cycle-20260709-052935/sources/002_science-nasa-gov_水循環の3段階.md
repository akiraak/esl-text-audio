"The water cycle is the path that all water follows as it moves around Earth."

"Water changes between three states of matter — solid, liquid, and gas — in the water cycle."

"Heat from sunlight causes water to evaporate from oceans, lakes, and streams. Evaporation occurs when liquid water turns into gaseous water vapor."

"Water from plants and trees also evaporates into the atmosphere. This is called transpiration."

"The water vapor to turn back into liquid water in a process known as condensation. When this happens, it creates clouds."

"When a cloud becomes full of liquid water, it falls from the sky as rain or snow — also known as precipitation."
