# Lost in a Small Town

Mia was on holiday in a small town in Europe. She wanted to find a famous cafe. She walked and walked, but her phone's map app suddenly died.

Mia looked around. All the streets looked the same. She walked left, then right, then left again. She was very tired, and she felt lost.

Then she walked into a quiet street. She had never been there before, and she felt nervous. Nobody was there. Mia stopped and took a deep breath.

Suddenly, a woman with a small dog walked by. "Are you okay?" the woman asked kindly. Mia said, "I'm lost. I can't find the cafe." The woman smiled and said, "I know a shortcut. Follow me!"

They walked together and talked. The woman lived in the town, so she knew all the streets very well. Soon, they reached the cafe.

Inside, Mia thanked the woman and bought her a cup of tea. The owner laughed and said, "She comes here every day! You made a new friend today." Mia smiled. Getting lost was not so bad after all.
